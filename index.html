About me

I was born on November 28th in Nablus, Palestine. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at Birzeit University.

My journey into coding began in 2019 when I wrote my first code in Python. Initially, I worked on developing simple games using Python's Turtle module. Subsequently, I delved into learning C++ and honed my problem-solving skills on platforms like Codeforces. This marked the inception of my competitive programming journey, transitioning from a high school student tackling basic problems to becoming a champion in local competitions and achieving recognition as a medalist in regional and international competitions.

In 2020, I ventured into web development, embarking on various projects that allowed me to hone my skills. I expanded my expertise by delving into React Native and React.js during that period. As a result, I have evolved into a proficient front-end developer with a portfolio comprising innovative projects and an internship experience at Foothill Technology Solutions, further enriching my professional journey.
